East Meets West 2023

Meet Team Jalwa

This is Team Jalwa, the EMW2023 fashion act.

Watch our full performance here

Being a part of the show in my first year of university and with the EMW fashion act being grander than ever before, it was an incredible experience to make new friendships and create unforgettable memories. Within two months, our act was brought to life by our three incredible choreographers (Mila, Rohil and Nikhil), who also gave me the opportunity to assist them with the choreography of some of the dance acts we performed. It was overall a truly rewarding experience to be a part of the team and to receive all the support for contributing my ideas and choreographing dance pieces to make our act performance even better.
